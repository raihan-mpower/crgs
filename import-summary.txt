------------

Eclipse projects. Here's how the projects were restructured:

* libs/android-support-v4.jar => actionbarsherlock_prev/libs/android-support-v4.jar
* lint.xml => actionbarsherlock_prev/lint.xml
* res/ => actionbarsherlock_prev/src/main/res/
* src/ => actionbarsherlock_prev/src/main/java/
